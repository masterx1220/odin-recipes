Ground Beef Skillet for the soul

This tasty, easy all in one ground beef dish will fill you right up

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Vodka sauce
  • Rice (either jasmin or white)
  • Taco bell con Queso
  • Half and half

Spices

  • Minced garlic
  • Tajin
  • Onion powder
  • Chili
  • Chili lime
  • Roasted garlic and herb seasoning
  • Garlic pepper
  • Meat tenderizer

Directions

  1. Using a skillet add the ground beef and spices, and then using medium heat brown the beef.
  2. Once the ground beef is cooked drain the meat using a strainer.
  3. Retun the meat to the skillet add the desired water and uncook rice.
  4. Cook the rice until the water is just about gone.
  5. Next add your vodka sauce, half and half, and queso.
  6. Cook down the sauce to right consistency.
  7. Enjoy!
